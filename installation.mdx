Are you unsure how to install CafePOS Lite in your studio? Then you've come to the right place.

<Card title="Downloading Notice" type="info">
  As our website is currently inactive—due to an upcoming complete frontend and backend rework for v2—the product files can currently be found only on the official Discord server.
</Card>

---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Downloading Your Product">
    Visit our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/s4k6VgNw) and look for the "NGS Products" category. There should be a channel there named `cafepos-lite.` In that channel, you will find a file available for download named **NGS CafePOS Lite v1.0.2**. Download it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Roblox Studio">
    Open Roblox Studio and select your experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert The File">
    Select the workspace and right-click. (On a MacBook, tap the touchpad with two fingers.) Select "Insert" -\> "Import Roblox Model." From there, select the file you downloaded from the Discord server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finished">
    You have successfully installed Cafe POS Lite into your experience. In the [Configuration](https://nextgenretailsystems.mintlify.app/configuration) section, you will find out how to configure your POS.
  </Step>
</Steps>
